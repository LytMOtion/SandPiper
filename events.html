Events at Sandpiper

Gather at the edge of the Pacific.

A public golf club on the Goleta bluffs — and a setting for the days worth marking. Weddings and celebrations, company gatherings, tournaments, and private dinners, with the ocean as the backdrop and the Grill in the kitchen.

The advantage here isn't a ballroom — it's the land: coastal bluff, open water, marine light, and evenings that end at sunset.

The setting

The venue is the coastline.

Eighteen holes falling toward the water, the ocean stretch on the back nine, and marine light that turns gold at the end of the day. It reads as Sandpiper because it is — not a banquet room dressed up with a view.

Capacities, layouts, and available spaces are set with the events team. Capacity & spaces to confirm
