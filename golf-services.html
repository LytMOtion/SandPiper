Golf services

Play the course better.

Two ways to sharpen your game at Sandpiper: personal instruction from the golf staff, and custom club fitting — both on the same coastal course you play. Lessons are built to you, and playing lessons happen out on the holes themselves.

To schedule either, contact the golf shop at 805.968.1541 — instruction ext. 4.

Instruction

A program built to your game.

Whether you're new to the game or a low handicap, the instruction team builds an individual plan around where you want to get to — and works with you on every part of it.

  • Full swing
  • Putting
  • Chipping
  • Pitching
  • Bunker play
  • On-course strategy
  • Club fitting
  • Action plan
